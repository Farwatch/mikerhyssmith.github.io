who we are and what we do

Lets start with the vehicle

A vehicle can come new from the manufacturer or can be a used vehicle and already listed on AutoTrader.


Where does it go next?

The vehicle can be sold straight to the consumer or make its way to a retailer's forecourt.

Customer acquires a vehicle

We have invested lots of time and effort into making sourcing vehicles easier and more efficient for our customers. We have multiple products available to our customers to aid in the process.


find out who helps make this happen

AutoTrade-Mail (ATM)

Dealer to dealer platform created by dealers for dealers that allows them trade vehicles at trade prices.

i-Control

There is a reason we class ourselves as a data company above anything else. i-Control utilises this data to allow our dealers to buy the right stock and sell it at the right price to maximise profits.

i-Control links closely with...

Part Exchange

A great tool allowing you to compare stock in the market against vehicles on AutoTrader, with similar cars advertised available to view in an instant.
